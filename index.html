How it works

Three steps.
One swap.

No interviews, no payments, no middleman. Just two people who want what the other knows.

01
List what you know
Post the skill you can teach and what you want to learn back. Be specific — "React hooks for beginners" beats just "programming."
02
Find your match
Search by skill, city, or level. When someone's offer matches your want, send a swap request with a short message.
03
Learn together
Set a schedule, meet on video or chat, and start teaching each other. Rate your swap — your rating is your reputation here.
